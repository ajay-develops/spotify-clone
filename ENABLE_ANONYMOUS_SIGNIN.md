# Enable Anonymous Sign-Ins in Supabase

## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Go to your Supabase Dashboard**
   - Visit: https://app.supabase.com
   - Select your project: `spotify-clone` (or your project name)

2. **Navigate to Authentication Settings**
   - Click on **"Authentication"** in the left sidebar
   - Click on **"Providers"** in the submenu

3. **Enable Anonymous Sign-Ins**
   - Scroll down to find **"Anonymous"** in the providers list
   - Toggle the switch to **enable** Anonymous sign-ins
   - Click **"Save"** or the setting should auto-save

4. **Verify the Setting**
   - Make sure the Anonymous provider shows as **enabled** (green toggle)

5. **Test in Your App**
   - Go back to your app
   - Click "Sign in Anonymously (Demo)"
   - It should now work without errors!
